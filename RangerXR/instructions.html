Instructions for sharing KeyShotXR

Below is your KeyShotXR. To view the KeyShotXR by itself open the RangerXR.html file in the RangerXR folder.

 

Uploading to and displaying on your website:

Upload the 'RangerXR' folder with all of its contents to your webserver. Copy (Ctrl-C/Cmd-C) and Paste (Ctrl-V/Cmd-V) the code below to your webpage.

[Code]: 

<div style="max-width: 1694px; max-height: 1133px;">
  <div style="left: 0px; width: 100%; height: 0px; position: relative; padding-bottom: 66.8831%; overflow: hidden;">
    <iframe src="RangerXR.html"
                 allowfullscreen
                 style="position: absolute; top: 0px; left: 0px; height: 100%; width: 1px; min-width: 100%; *width: 100%;"
                 frameborder="0"
                 scrolling="no">
    </iframe>
  </div>
</div>

If you share your KeyShotXR via email, send the entire zipped 'RangerXR' folder to the recipient(s).
